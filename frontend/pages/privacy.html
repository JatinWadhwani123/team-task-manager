Privacy Policy

We explain what information TaskFlow Pro collects, why we collect it, and the choices you have when using the service.

Effective: May 16, 2026 Applies to all TaskFlow Pro users

Overview

TaskFlow Pro collects only the information needed to create accounts, manage projects and tasks, keep the product secure, and improve the experience for teams.

What we collect

  • Account details: name, email address, and login information.
  • Workspace content: projects, tasks, members, due dates, and status updates you add.
  • Usage and device data: browser type, pages visited, and basic diagnostics.
  • Cookies: session and preference data used to keep the site reliable.

How we use information

We use information to operate the service, personalize your workspace, protect accounts from unauthorized access, respond to support requests, and understand which features need improvement.

Sharing and retention

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with trusted service providers that help us host, secure, analyze, or support TaskFlow Pro. We keep information only as long as needed for service, security, legal, or legitimate business purposes.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal information. To make a request, contact support from the email address connected to your account.
