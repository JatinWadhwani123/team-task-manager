Cookie Policy

This policy explains how TaskFlow Pro uses cookies and similar technologies to keep sessions working, remember preferences, and improve the product.

Effective: May 16, 2026 Part of our privacy practices

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. TaskFlow Pro uses them to recognize sessions, remember preferences, and understand how the service is performing.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: required for login, authentication, and core site functions.
  • Preference cookies: remember choices such as interface settings.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand usage patterns and improve features.

Controlling cookies

You can block or remove cookies through your browser settings. Some essential cookies are required for TaskFlow Pro to work, including account sign-in and workspace navigation.

Policy updates

We may update this policy when our tools, product features, or legal requirements change. The effective date above will reflect the latest version.
